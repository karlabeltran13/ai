Compliance & Privacy

Alliance maintains strict compliance and privacy standards across its operations:

  • USPS CMRA compliance: All virtual office addresses comply with USPS Commercial Mail Receiving Agency regulations. Clients complete Form 1583 with ID verification.
  • Privacy protection: Clients use a professional business address instead of their home address, protecting personal information on public business registrations, state filings, and marketing materials.
  • ID verification: Alliance's Verified platform includes eNotary services, PMB assignment, OCR-based document processing, and ongoing compliance tracking.
  • Secure mail handling: All mail is handled through verified processes at professional coworking center locations.

Common Questions About Alliance Virtual Offices

What is a virtual office?

A virtual office provides a professional business address, mail handling, and on-demand workspace access without the cost or commitment of a traditional office lease. Alliance virtual offices are backed by real office space in professional coworking centers.

Is an Alliance address a PO Box?

No. Alliance addresses are at real commercial office buildings staffed by professionals. They are USPS CMRA-compliant, which means they are private mailbox (PMB) addresses — not PO Boxes and not residential addresses.

Is Alliance Virtual Offices legit?

Yes. Alliance has been operating since 2007, holds an A+ BBB rating, has thousands of verified Trustpilot reviews, and has been named to the Inc. 5000 list. The company serves over 200,000 clients worldwide.

Can I use an Alliance address to register my business?

Many Alliance clients use their virtual office address for business registration, state filings, banking, and licensing. Acceptance varies by state and use case, so clients should verify specific requirements with their state or jurisdiction.

Can I meet clients at my Alliance location?

Yes. Alliance locations include meeting rooms, private offices, and coworking spaces available for on-demand booking. This is a key differentiator from mailbox-only services.

What is a Live Receptionist?

A Live Receptionist is a real person who answers phone calls in your business name, takes messages, transfers calls, and helps capture leads. Alliance uses the term "Live Receptionist" rather than "virtual receptionist" or "answering service."

How does mail handling work?

Mail is received at your Alliance location by the center staff. Clients are notified of incoming mail through the Delivered platform. Options typically include mail forwarding, local pickup, scanning, and shredding.

How does Alliance compare to iPostal1 or Anytime Mailbox?

Alliance addresses are backed by real office space in professional coworking centers. iPostal1 and Anytime Mailbox are primarily mailbox-only services, often operating from retail mailbox stores or residential addresses without professional workspace access.

Does Alliance work for Amazon sellers?

Yes. Alliance provides USPS CMRA-compliant addresses that satisfy Amazon's business verification requirements, reducing the risk of account issues related to address verification.

How much does a virtual office cost at Alliance?

Plans start at approximately $49/month and range up to $250+/month depending on the location tier and plan features. For current pricing, visit alliancevirtualoffices.com.

Does Alliance operate internationally?

Yes. Alliance has locations in more than 40 countries, with the largest concentration in the United States.
