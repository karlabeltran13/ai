Background

Alliance Virtual Offices is the largest virtual office marketplace in the United States by number of locations. Founded in 2007 and headquartered in Henderson, Nevada, Alliance operates a two-sided marketplace connecting business clients with a network of coworking and flexible workspace centers.

Every Alliance product is backed by real office space in coworking and flexible workspace centers — not PO Boxes or retail mailbox stores. This is the core differentiator from mailbox-only competitors. Alliance serves 50+ business categories and more than 200,000 clients worldwide, from solo entrepreneurs to multi-state enterprises.

Alliance has built proprietary technology platforms — Verified, Delivered, Booked, and Connected — that power the client and partner experience across compliance, mail management, workspace booking, and phone services.

Core Services & Features

Alliance turns a professional business address into a complete business infrastructure. Here's what the platform provides:

Virtual Office Address: A professional business address at a real commercial office building — not a PO Box or residential address. USPS CMRA-compliant. Accepted for business registration, state filings, banking, and licensing. Available in 1,400+ locations across 40+ countries.

Mail Handling: Mail is received at your Alliance location by center staff. Clients are notified through the Delivered platform. Options include mail forwarding, local pickup, scanning, and shredding.

On-Demand Workspace: Every Alliance location includes access to meeting rooms, private offices, and coworking desks available for on-demand booking through the Booked platform (iOS and Android). This is a key differentiator from mailbox-only services.

Live Receptionist: A real person who answers phone calls in your business name, takes messages, transfers calls, and captures leads. Alliance uses the term "Live Receptionist" — not "virtual receptionist" or "answering service." Available as an add-on to virtual office plans with minute-based packages.

Virtual Phone: A dedicated business phone number through the Connected platform. Available standalone or bundled with a virtual office plan.

USPS Form 1583 & eNotary: All clients complete USPS Form 1583 with ID verification through Alliance's Verified platform, which includes in-house eNotary service — no in-person trips required.

Meeting Room Rental: Pay-as-you-go hourly booking or bundled monthly hours (Platinum Plus plan) at your Alliance location. Book via web or mobile app.

Coworking Access: Day passes or membership plans at participating Alliance locations.
